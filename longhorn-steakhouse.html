Our Heritage

Fire, Craft &
Western Soul

Born from a passion for the open range and the craft of the grill, LongHorn Steakhouse has been bringing the spirit of the American West to every table since 1981. Our Davenport location continues that proud tradition — a place where every guest is welcomed like an old friend.

Our chefs source only the freshest, hand-selected cuts, dry-aged to perfection and fire-kissed over real flames. From our legendary Outlaw Ribeye to our seasonal specials, every dish reflects our unwavering commitment to quality, flavor, and heart.
